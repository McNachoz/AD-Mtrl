Gyanaranjan Mishra (PhD Student)

"I am a 2nd year Ph.D. student in the Alloy Design and Materials Testing Research Laboratory under the mechanical engineering department. I did my bachelor's from National Institute of Technology, Rourkela, and my master's from Indian Institute of Technology, Bombay, both my previous degrees are in the field of metallurgical and materials science engineering. I am the recipient of the institute medal for being the best metallurgist during my bachelor's. I have worked as a principal researcher in research and development department of Tata Steel Limited, India, where my research focused on developing new products for automotive, line pipe, and construction applications. Currently, I am working in collaboration with Natural Resources Canada - CanmetMATERIALS towards developing high-performance electrical steel which can be used for aero-engine applications. Apart from being a researcher, I am an avid saltwater aquarist and orchidist."
