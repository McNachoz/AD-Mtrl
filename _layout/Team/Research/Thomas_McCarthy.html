Thomas McCarthy (Masters Student)

"Hi, my name is Thomas, and I am currently pursuing my Master of Science in Mechanical Engineering. Prior to beginning graduate studies, I completed a Bachelor of Mechanical Engineering (Mechatronics option) with a minor in Mathematics at UNB. During undergraduate studies I had the opportunity to complete three co-op placements with the AD-MTRL group where I focused on developing constitutive models for various materials. My current research focuses on leveraging regression-based machine-learning models to optimize the additive manufacturing process of Nickel-based alloys. I hope that with my research I can help promote the further adoption of additive manufacturing in Atlantic Canadian industries."
