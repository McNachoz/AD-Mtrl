Felisters Zvavamwe (PhD Student)

"Hi, Im Felisters. I graduated with a Bachelor of Science in Metallurgical Engineering from the University of Zimbabwe in 2015 . I then pursued a master of science in Metallurgical and Materials Engineering at Marmara University, Turkey in November 2019. After completing my masters studies I joined the University of Zimbabwe and worked as an instructor until December 2022. I then moved to Canada to start my doctoral studies in January 2023. I am working on the designing of medium manganese steels which are 3rd generation Advanced High Strength Steels (AHSS) for automotive applications. I am also investigating the effect of microalloying these steels with vanadium and molybdenum on the equilibrium phases present."
