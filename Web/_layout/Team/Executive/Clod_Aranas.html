Dr. Clodualdo Aranas Jr., PEng (Associate Professor)

I am currently an Associate Professor and a Principal Investigator in the Mechanical Engineering Department at the University of New Brunswick. My primary role involves collaborating closely with private industry to provide support for their research and development endeavors. Additionally, I have the responsibility of fostering the growth of Canada's economy by ensuring that students are trained based on the current trends and technology. My team’s research interests are in the area of physical and mechanical metallurgy, with a focus on materials characterization and mechanical testing. We are active in publishing our research findings in peer-reviewed journals – you can check our publication history for details! Feel free to contact me if you are interested in working with Alloy Design and Materials Testing Research Laboratory! I am also open to discussing potential projects and start-up ideas!
